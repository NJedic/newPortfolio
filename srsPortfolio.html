Screen Rant Scrape

Previous Next

Screen Rant Scrape allows the user to scrape the latest movie news articles from ScreenRant.com, save their favorite articles, and even make notes on them! The application uses Cheerio to scrape the news, and MongoDB to store the articles and their respective notes. The notes themselves can be deleted once they've served their purpose or updated to include new information.

I wanted the design to be as graphic as possible and reminiscent of a bold, high-contrast comic book illustration, while still remaining simple, clean, & modern. Using gradients and playing with opaque vs. translucent modules, allowed me to achieve the desired effect. Implementing the Bootstrap card component to display the scraped articles, gives them a layered look at the bottom of the application, much like a stack of comic books.
