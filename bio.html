A multi-media artist with Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree from Mason Gross School of the Arts at Rutgers University, I have been in the creative field for most of my life. Working with textile dyeing, color matching, and costume crafting for Broadway and Television helped me to hone my design skills and understand the integral part color and texture play in conjuring up a particular mood or feeling.

Shortly thereafter, I spent over two years working in customer service for a luxury retail brand. I learned an immense amount not only in regards to marketing, but also customer interaction and expectation. Retail allowed me to take a glimpse into the minds of our customers on a daily basis and judge, on the ground level, how well a certain promotional or marketing campaign was working. I was not only responsible for the success of our store, but also the hiring of its staff and the training of all employees, giving me a crash course in the importance of teamwork and trust above all else.

In my career now as a Front End & UI/UX Designer, I find I am able to use my entire skillset in order to build diverse, interactive, innovative, evocative, and user-friendly designs for a wide-rang of clientele.
