BreakPoint Collective

Previous Next

The inspiration for the BreakPoint Collective site came from the Artist herself. Her extensive portfolio born from her simple love of the sea was my jumping off point for color and font.

The Enter page is minimalist, but dynamic, with a piece of the client's art taking center stage. The image itself was enhanced to magnify its color and clarity. The addition of a small amount of animation steered the page away from looking stagnant and flat.

The Main page continues as an extension of the artist, with her works and photographs taking center stage. Small pops of color dot the page, but nothing too obvious as to take away from the overall aesthetic.

As she works on launching her Etsy store and upping her social media presence, the client wanted something that could be a landing page of sorts for anyone looking for more information on her background and the work itself. The site will continue to grow more robust as the client continues her journey. This page is definite work in progress.
