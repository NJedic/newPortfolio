Harry Potter Trivia Game

Previous Next

The Harry Potter Trivia Game is meant to simulate the written portion of a Hogwarts Student's O.W.L. Potions Examination. The questions are purposefully and particularly difficult (even for the most dedicated fan) and the user has a very short amount of time to answer as many questions as they can before the questions disappear.

Designing this application was particularly thrilling. It's meant to be as evocative of being in an actual Potions classroom as possible - dark, ominous, and oozing. The scroll on the opening page seems to be floating just underneath the foreboding text, as if suspended there by magic. On the test page itself, the unfurled scroll background was custom lengthened to accommodate all of the questions.

The typeface was chosen very carefully; students and faculty are required to write with a quill pen, and this font looks like modern calligraphy. It has a flourish, but it is still legible, as I imagine the hand of the experienced witch or wizard who wrote the test would look.

After two minutes, the user's time is up, and they will be shown their score, regardless of how many questions were actually answered. Feel free to try your luck, if you dare!
