glimpse

Glimpse is a full-stack MERN application that allows users to make an account, create a shared calendar with their friends, family, or coworkers, and add all of their events to one central space.

The application is a snapshot of the days, weeks, and months ahead and allows users to see when their fellow group members are free to hang out or to get away.

Our design team began the process with a vintage advertisement. We were drawn to the colors and the feel of the image itself, how bold and evocative it was. In order to make glimpse successful, we juxtaposed those nostalgic colors - peach, teal, & goldenrod - with splashy, modern images of people out on the town and returning to nature. The blurry city image and our use of sheer colors vs. opaque helped jumpstart our app into the modern era, keeping it fresh, while our use of rounded edges on our buttons, images, and other sundry items pulled the design together - hearkening back to the clean, rounded lines of eras past.
