PARK IT!

Previous Next

PARK IT! is an easy-to-use application that allows the user to search for upcoming outdoor activities by location & type. The site also uses supporting APIs to display the weather and plot the locations of the various search results on a map.

Our design team wanted something that didn't feel flat-out woodsy and rugged. We started with the typeface and went from there - using it's kitchy, retro campsite feel as a starting point for the rest of our design. The birch wood background and image of silhouetted trees we used for the header firmly ground the application in the modern era, while also serving as our color inspiration. Animation was used on this project to add to it's fun, playful appeal.
