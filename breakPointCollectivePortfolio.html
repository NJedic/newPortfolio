BreakPoint Collective

Previous Next

The inspiration for the BreakPoint Collective site came from the client herself. Her extensive portfolio born from her simple love of the sea was my jumping off point for color and font. We wanted a design that was brimming with nostalgia, but still felt fresh and like a natural extension of her work.

The finished product is reminiscent of the client's personal workspace - covered in scattered photos, scraps of paper with scribbled ideas, and abounding in tangible color inspo. All of the images used are the client's own, but photoshpped into polaroids for a twist on a typical photo spread.

As she works on launching her Etsy store and upping her social media presence, it was imperative the website served as a hub for anyone looking for more information on her process as well as any upcoming shows.
