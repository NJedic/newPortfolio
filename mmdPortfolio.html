My Medical Diary

Previous Next

My Medical Diary, or MMD, is an application for storing Medical Notes, Appointments, To-Do Lists, & Doctors all in one place with an easy-to-use UI. It's the perfect program for patients and caretakers alike as it makes it incredibly simple to relay all of the pertinent information related to any illness to anyone - doctor or otherwise - without having to carry folders full of paperwork or store everything in your memory. As a bonus, any data or doctor entered into MMD can be both updated or deleted as circumstances change.

The concept for MMD came out of the simple desire to make a calming, accessible app for people to use in times of high stress. Our design team pulled our color inspiration from a photograph of a tropical wave breaking at twilight. Those light blue hues, semi-transparent modules, and an easy-to-read but sophisticated typeface are topped off with simple images and user-friendly modals. It's the perfect marriage of functionality and design.
